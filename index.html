Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is known for its golden beaches, lush hills, and ancient cities. The island offers a mix of nature and history that attracts travelers from all over the world.

Sigiriya or Sinhagiri (Lion Rock Sinhala: සීගිරිය, Tamil: சிகிரியா/சிங்ககிரி, pronounced SEE-gi-ri-yə) is an ancient rock fortress located in the northern Matale District near the town of Dambulla in the Central Province

Galle Fort, Kandy, Ella, and Nuwara Eliya are great too.
